## Analysis

Customize this skill for your specific domain. A good skill tells the agent:

1. **What to investigate** — the scope and constraints
2. **How to gather data** — which connections and endpoints to use
3. **How to reason** — the analytical framework to apply
4. **How to present findings** — the format and level of detail
5. **What to avoid** — common pitfalls and anti-patterns
